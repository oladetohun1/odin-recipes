Lasagna

Description

It takes a little work, but it is worth it

It takes about 50 minutes to cook in an oven

Ingredient

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 1 (16 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil
  • (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and olive oil; cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef and sausage in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease. Stir mushrooms, garlic salt, oregano, thyme, and basil into meat mixture; cook and stir until mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes. Stir tomato sauce into mixture; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer until flavors blend, about 20 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese, eggs, and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl.
  5. Spoon a layer of meat sauce into the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish; add a layer of noodles. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese mixture over noodles; top with a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat layering process 3 more times, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbling, about 30 minutes.
