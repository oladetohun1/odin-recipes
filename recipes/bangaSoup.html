Banga Soup

Description

Banga Soup is a Nigeria soup made from plantain soup

It takes about 1 hour to cook

Ingredient

  • 1 kg of fresh palm fruits or 800g of tinned palm fruit concentrate
  • Beef
  • Shaki (cow tripe)
  • Smoked fish
  • Stock fish
  • 1 tablespoon ground crayfish
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1-2 Scotch bonnet peppers
  • 1 handful of scent leaves
  • 1 handful of bitter leaves
  • 1-2 big stock cubes
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and boil the palm fruits till they are very soft. If you are using tinned palm fruit concentrate, skip to step 2.
  2. When the palm fruits are soft, pound them in a mortar till the flesh is separated from the nut.
  3. Boil some water and pour on the pounded palm fruits. Stir and strain the juice into a pot. If you are using tinned palm fruit concentrate, just pour it into a pot.
  4. Boil the palm fruit juice till the oil starts to float at the top. You will also notice that the juice has thickened. This should take about 20 minutes.
  5. Add the beef, shaki, stock fish, onions and stock cubes and cook till done. You may need to add more water while cooking the meat.
  6. Add the smoked fish, crayfish, pepper and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the scent leaves, bitter leaves and salt to taste. Stir very well and simmer for 2 minutes.
  8. Remove from heat and serve with any swallow of your choice.
